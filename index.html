F.A.Q

  • Don't Have a Duothan partner?

    Register through this Google Form. Every individual who is not having a partner to participate duothan 1.0 can register here. We will connecting you with a random individual just like you. Get yourselves registered before 12 Febuary 6.00 AM. The following conditions may apply.

    • - Your information including your Name, Contact Number, and Email address will be shared with the relevant teammate which we are teaming you up to participate in this hackathon.
    • - You cannot quit the hackathon once after we assigned you a teammate.
    • - Once we informed you about your teammate, it is your responsibility to keep in contact with the assigned individual and decide a name for your TEAM.
    • - You should immediately report the Team Name to the Organizing Committee before 11:59 PM, 12th February, or your team will not be eligible to participate in the hackathon.
    • - Please note that we are only assigning the individual to complete this hackathon.

  • What is the flow of the hackathon?

    The hackathon starts at 9.00 AM on 13 February. You need to login to the hackerrank website using the credentials we provide. There may be 15 or more questions on different levels (Easy, Intermediate, Hard) to solve within 12 hours. All you have to do is grab a coffee and hack the Duothan with your partner.

  • Why participate?

    This is a fabulous occasion to pair up with your partner and hack the heart out this February. You will get unlimited coding experience on various programming problems. Hack this valentine with your partner and stand a chance to win cool swags and certificates.

  • How to reach us?

    You will be added to a whatsapp group before the day of the hack. You can always reach out to the organizing committee through that group. If you have any questions before the day of the hack, contact us via IEEE NSBM facebook page. Or drop a email at nsbmieee@gmail.com.
